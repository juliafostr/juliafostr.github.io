Madison Marsh, Miss America 2024

Someone who inspires me is Madison Marsh. She is the 2024 Miss America winner! The 22 year old active-duty air force officer is the first officer to ever compete in the competition. She is a very smart Harvard student who had her own foundation, the Whitney Marsh Foundation to fund pancreatic cancer research. Mash inspires me to continually pursue my education and to always aim high. I’m excited to see where her career takes her! Who knows what she’ll achieve!
