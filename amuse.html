Meaningles Scrolling

by Jules

FMLife.com

I discovered this site during the Pandemic's push for doom scrolling and wasted time to get through quarentine. The brutal honesty of these posts is often laughable, as well as relatable.
There is a voting system that I dislike though, which has randoms decide if "You deserved it" and "I agree, your life sucks".

Click the link FMLife.com for an amusing scroll!

Youtube.com

The classic early 2,000s site to watch your favorite clips of ultimate fails, cute kitten videos, and of course, your funniest youtubers!
There is also a voting system associated with youtube such as clicking "like" or "dislike".

Click the link youtube.com for another amusing scroll!
