He finally owns his nickname?

Dwayne, the rock Johnson is a household name. The famous WWE fighter-turned actor has argued he owns the name, and recently bought the rights to the famous "the Rock" nickname. "Johnson, a movie and wrestling star, has a business portfolio that includes his production company Seven Bucks Productions, tequila brand Teremana Tequila, energy drink company ZOA Energy, Project Rock apparel brand and the United Football League." Now, the name is only for him, THE ROCK!
